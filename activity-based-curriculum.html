Our ACT Program

Activity-Based Curriculum for Tacit Knowledge (ACT)

Activities, both worksheets and physical games, are crucial in early child development programs. Many researchers advocate activity-based learning as the most effective method for preschool skill development. However, these programs often fail to deliver desired outcomes due to unstructured implementation and the lack of a proper curriculum.

This course, entitled “Activity-Based Curriculum for Tacit Knowledge (ACT) for Preschool,” provides a foundational understanding of curriculum-associated skill development among preschools. It empowers educators to craft structured and effective activity-based curriculums that ensure impactful learning.

Key Highlights of the ACT Program

The ACT program focuses on equipping participants with the knowledge and tools to:

Understand activity-based learning and curriculum for preschools

Implement the curriculum easily in preschools

Develop your own activity-based curriculum

Create effective activity sheets for preschool children

With this program, educators gain practical skills to design and execute impactful activities that support comprehensive preschool learning, ensuring immediate and meaningful implementation.
