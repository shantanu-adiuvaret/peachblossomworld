Nursery Teachers Training Program

Empowering Innovation, Delivering Excellence

Adiuvaret Research & Education Foundation (AREF) is an organisation incorporated with Govt. of India to focus on research and education aspects of child development including establishing best practices in skill building across the globe. In collaboration with D Y Patil University we have developed comprehensive 6 month training programs and 1 year certificate courses of NTT- Nursery Teachers Training which will help early childhood teachers in India to use innovative methods of skill building.
