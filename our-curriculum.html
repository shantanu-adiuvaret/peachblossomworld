Our Curriculum

Holistic Development for Every Child

The National Policy on Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) 2013 envisions "nurturance and promotion of holistic development and active learning of all children below 6 years of age by promoting free, universal, inclusive, equitable, joyful, and contextualized opportunities." The early years are when a child’s brain development is at its fastest pace. Communication skills and social/personal habits are also forming during this time.

Supported by a team of experts, our curriculum ensures a strong foundation for the child’s all-around development. We follow a 5*4 skill-based curriculum to nurture the various aspects of a child's growth.
