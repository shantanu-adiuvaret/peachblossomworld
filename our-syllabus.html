Our Preschool Syllabus

Fostering Lifelong Learning and Holistic Development

Good quality preschool syllabi have been shown to have a positive impact on children becoming interested in lifelong learning. Children pass through an identifiable sequence of developmental milestones in various domains. Hence, the syllabus requires careful planning to create a stimulating and enriching environment, allowing each child to develop their full potential.

Our activities are designed to ignite curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child. The syllabus is carefully crafted based on the suggestions provided by the **ECCE (Early Childhood Care and Education)** and **NCERT (National Council of Educational Research and Training)**, in line with the Government of India's new education policy for the pre-primary level of education and skill building.

Aligned with ECCE & NCERT Guidelines

We focus on the following key aspects in our syllabus:

Age-appropriate learning: Stimulates curiosity and creativity.

Incorporation of ECCE and NCERT recommendations: Focused on skill building.

Promoting holistic development: Including cognitive, physical, emotional, and social growth.

Fostering a love for learning: That lasts a lifetime.

Skill-Based Progression Through Different Age Groups

Our syllabus is tailored for the following age groups, ensuring a smooth transition from one level to the next:

Play Group (2-3 years): Focuses on sensory activities, basic motor skills, and emotional development.

Nursery (3-4 years): Introduction to simple concepts of language, numbers, shapes, and social skills.

LKG (4-5 years): Reinforcement of language skills, early mathematics, and physical activities.

UKG (5-6 years): Advanced language development, basic reading and writing skills, and problem-solving activities.

The entire syllabus has been structured progressively, ensuring that each child develops essential skills at every stage.
